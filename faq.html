Frequent Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • How can you print a document from your laptop at HZ?

    • You can print a document from your laptop by visiting print.hz.nl After authenticating, you can upload the file you would like to print. Optionally you can choose whether you want to print in color or not. Select the HZ Printer or HZ Plotter to request the print job. Now you can Register using your HZ Pass on the TouchID next to one of the printers. Select "Print / Afdrukken" in the menu and your document will be printed.

  • What do you need to do when you are sick / show symptoms of coronavirus?

    • Inform the school that you are sick or show symptoms of covid-19 and stay home.
  • How can you book a project space in one of the wings?

    • Go to MyHZ > selfservice Portal > new booking > Project Space > choose your time and place A quicklink: Selfservice Portal

  • Where to park your car?

    • The HZ has a new parking locaton located near Het Groene Woud in Middelburg. There is a parking site on Poelendaelsesingel 10 with 100 available parking spots for students, staff and visitors. Students and staff can use your HZ-pass to access the parking lot. Follow the signs to the HZ parking spots that have a white sign with the HZ logo on it. Visitors need to be registered by employees or students beforehand with the mention of "Please park on HZ terrain". When arrived the visitor needs to report via the intercom. There are 2 more parking spots located directly infront of the HZ building for disabled. The use of these can be coordinated with the reception.
